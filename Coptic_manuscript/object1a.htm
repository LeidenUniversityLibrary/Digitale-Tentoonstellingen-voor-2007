Catalogue


O.Leid.Inv. 1
H x W = 12.5 x 6 cm. Provenance: unknown, acquired with Inv. 2, which originates from Deir el-Gizáz. Content: list of martyrs of Sebastia. Published: P. L.Bat. XXV 12.

O.Leid.Inv. 2 (+ O.Deir el-Gizáz inv. 41)
H x W = 10.9 x 8.9 + 12.5 x 11.2 cm. Provenance: Deir el-Gizáz. Content: writing exercise in the ‘Biblical Majuscule’. Published: P. L.Bat. XXV 11.
O.Leid.Inv. 40
H x W = 6 x 8 cm. Content: six lines of writing, very faded.

O.Leid.Inv. 48
H x W = 5.4 x 6.8 cm. Content: five or six lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side pitched.

O.Leid.Inv. 49
H x W = 8.5 x 6.8 cm. Content: seven lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side pitched.

O.Leid.Inv. 50
H x W = 9 x 7.5 cm. Content: ten lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side unpitched.

O.Leid.Inv. 51
H x W = 7.2 x 7.3 cm. Content: six lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side unpitched.

O.Leid.Inv. 52
H x W = 5.6 x 4.8 cm. Content: six lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side pitched; l. 3 mentions cheese.

O.Leid.Inv. 53
H x W = 4.8 x 6.1 cm. Content: five lines of writing on ribbed pottery, inner side unpitched; in l. 2 the personal name Kamoul.
