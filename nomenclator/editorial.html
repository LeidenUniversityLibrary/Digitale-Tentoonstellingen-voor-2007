Editorial note

In 1995, four hundred years after the original edition, a reprint of Bertius' Nomenclator appeared during the celebration of the four hundred and twentieth anniversary of the founding of Leiden University:

  • Petrus Bertius, Nomenclator. The first printed catalogue of Leiden University Library (1595). A facsimile edition with an introduction by R. Breugelmans and an authors' index compiled by Jan Just Witkam. Leiden 1995.

The website for the special collections of Leiden University Library offers a good opportunity to publish a re-edition based on the facsimile, this time in electronic form.

It has proved impossible to simply digitize the 1995 facsimile. Because the used copy (Leiden University Library, 1408 I 57) -- with the extra gatherings 2C and 3C, N-O, and addendum slips on E2r, F1v, G4r, H3r of the second issue -- was printed on very thin paper, the text shines through, thus affecting the legibility of the images. Therefore it has been decided to use a copy in the library of the first issue (1408 I 58) as a basis for digitization, and to add images of the extra gatherings and pages with addendum slips from copy 1408 I 57.

Besides the introduction and index the electronic edition has also been provided with a table of contents, in order to facilitate browsing.

The electronic version was prepared by A.Th. Bouwman and the Electronic Text Centre Leiden (ETCL).

R. Breugelmans
Jan Just Witkam
